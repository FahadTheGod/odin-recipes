Manakeesh

Description:

Manakeesh is a traditional Lebanese appetizer/breakfast that is also known as the Lebanese Pizza.

Manakeesh come in many different forms such as cheese, zaatar or beef manakeesh.

Ingredients:

  • Sweet Yeast Dough
  • Powdered Milk
  • Zaatar
  • Cheese
  • Beef

Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough with powdered milk.
  2. Add your ingredient of choice from cheese, zaatar or beef
  3. Add to the over for about 30 minutes
