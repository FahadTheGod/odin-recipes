Dolma

Description:

Dolma is a famous Iraqi dish that is very delicious.

Many different cultures make and serve it as an appetizer, but the Iraqi way is served as an actual meal!

Ingredients:

  • Grape Leaves
  • Rice
  • Onions
  • Lemons
  • Ground Lamb
  • Vegetables
  • Tomato Paste

Steps:

  1. Prepare and cut the vegetables and onions
  2. Open the grape leaves and start stuffing the leaves with rice, ground lamb and vegetables and onions
  3. Carefully roll the grape leaves tightly
  4. Add all of the rolled grape leaves into a pot
  5. Make sure all of the rolled grape leaves are squished together
  6. Add the lemons and the tomato paste
